Challenge Fund round 3 application

 

This form will take about 20-30 minutes to complete. We recommend that you review the guidance notes and look over a list of the questions that the form contains before you start.

Once you complete the form you will receive an email confirmation that it has been submitted successfully.

The closing date for applications is the Friday 4 September 2015. The link to this form will be removed from our website at 17:00 that day, so please make sure you leave yourself plenty of time to submit your application.

If you need any help with the form then please send an email explaining what the issue is to digital@scvo.org.uk.
