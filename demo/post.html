Post an offer / want

The Charter is all about getting organisations across Scotland to support each other to achieve the goal of ensuring that every citizen has the Basic Digital Skills needed today.

By posting an OFFER, you'll be making other organisations in Scotland aware of valuable resources that you're willing to contribute to the Charter.

Likewise, by posting a WANT, other Charter signatories can see what resources you need to support your great work and hopefully help you out.
